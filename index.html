What are the Gobos?

The Gobos are a popular awards event held annually to celebrate the Scratch projects from the past year. Any project within a year from the last deadline can be submitted.

How can I submit a project?

Submissions have not been opened yet. However, you can check out @GoboAwardsOfficial on Scratch, and a project will be released soon where you can nominate projects.
